Welcome to the homepage of the University of Waterloo Amateur Radio Club (VE3UOW)!

About the Club

You can find us on 446.100MHz with a CTCSS tone of 131.8Hz.
If you're on campus and a licensed amateur radio operator, give us a call.

Learn more about the club.

Joining the Club

If you are interested in joining the club, please contact us.

The club is open to all students of the University of Waterloo. An Amateur Radio License is not required.
